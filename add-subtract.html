Add

To bring two or more numbers (or things) together to make a new total. Here 1 ball is added to 1 ball to make 2 balls.

- http://mathisfun.com

Example:

When we add 2 and 3 we get 5.

2 + 3 = 5

Subtract

Subtraction is taking one number away from another.

- http://mathisfun.com

Example:

Start with 5 apples, then subtract 2. we are left with 3 apples.

5 - 2 = 3
